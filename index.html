Who I Am

About Dr. Zareena Sultana

Honorary Doctorate
Published Author
Community Leader

Prof. Dr. Zareena Sultana is an internationally recognized educator, author, and human rights advocate whose transformative journey spans over three decades of dedicated service to education and society.

Beginning her illustrious career at the International Indian School, Jeddah in 1990, Dr. Sultana has continuously evolved as a leader in education, touching thousands of lives across continents. Her transition from Saudi Arabia to India in 2009 marked a new chapter where she established herself as a pioneer in innovative teaching methodologies and holistic education.

Currently serving as the Dean of Students' Welfare at ISL Engineering College, Hyderabad, Dr. Sultana combines academic excellence with a deep commitment to student development, mental health, and inclusive education. Her expertise spans English Language Teaching, Soft Skills Development, Communication Excellence, and Educational Leadership.

My Story

Professional Journey

A remarkable journey of dedication, innovation, and impact across three decades

1990

The Beginning

Physical Education Teacher | International Indian School, Jeddah

Started my educational journey as a Physical Education teacher, laying the foundation for a career dedicated to holistic student development.

1990-2009

Leadership in Jeddah

Head of Department & Discipline In-Charge | IIS Jeddah

Led the Physical Education Department and served as Discipline In-Charge for over 5,000 students. Organized major events including India Festival, Women's Festival, and cultural programs as General Secretary.

  • Secretary, Indian Ladies Cultural Association
  • Organized cluster meets and international festivals
  • Received numerous awards for academic and cultural contributions
2009

New Horizons in India

English Language Educator

Transitioned to India and began teaching English in various schools, bringing international expertise to Indian education.

2010-2015

Educational Entrepreneurship

Franchise Owner | Sunshine Preschool & Bharatiya Vidyalaya

Acquired franchises of Sunshine Preschool and Day Care, and Bharatiya Vidyalaya College of Education and Training, pioneering early childhood education and teacher training.

2016

Turning Point

Associate Professor | ISL Engineering College, Hyderabad

Joined ISL Engineering College marking a transformative phase in my career. Organized literary and cultural events including debates, dramas, TEDx talks, and annual days.

2017-2020

Academic Advancement

Research Scholar | Himalayan Garhwal University

Pursued doctoral research in English Literature while continuing to excel in teaching and leadership roles. Completed multiple advanced certifications in TESOL, ELT, and NLP.

2020-2021

Distinguished Recognition

National Awards & Honorary Doctorate

Received Honorary Doctorate and prestigious Rashtra Prerna Award. Awarded Maulana Abul Kalam Azad Award for Excellence in Education. Completed Ph.D. in English.

2021-Present

Current Leadership

Dean of Students' Welfare | ISL Engineering College

Leading student welfare initiatives, promoting mental health, and fostering inclusive education. Appointed as Subject Expert (English) and Member of Board of Studies, JNTUH. Conducting webinars and guest lectures internationally.

  • Editor, Guinness World Record Book (2023)
  • Featured in CIO TIMES, TradeFloc, Business Talkz
  • Diamond Fellowship, Eudoxia University
  • Viksit Bharat Ambassador (2047)
